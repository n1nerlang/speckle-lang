#### CONTRIBUTING (sec. 0)
Thank you for contributing to Speckle! We are glad to welcome new contributors to our community. Your involvement plays a crucial role in shaping the future of our project. As a collaborative platform, we rely heavily on the input and creativity of individuals like you who share our passion for innovation and progress in technology.

##### SECTION 2


When you join us as a contributor, you become part of a vibrant, dynamic team dedicated to enhancing Speckle’s capabilities and functionalities. Whether you are a seasoned developer, a designer, or someone with a unique perspective, your contributions will help us grow and improve. We encourage you to share your ideas, feedback, and suggestions. Each piece of insight helps us build a better platform that meets the diverse needs of our users.

We understand that contributions come in various forms. If you have programming skills, you might choose to work directly on the codebase, adding new features or fixing existing bugs. If you possess design skills, your input in creating a user-friendly interface or improving user experience is invaluable. Additionally, if you are interested in documentation or community engagement, helping to create tutorials, guides, or leading discussions can provide great support and clarity for our users.
###### **SECTION 3**
Moreover, we believe in nurturing collaboration among our contributors. As part of the Speckle community, you will have opportunities to engage with others, share knowledge, and develop new skills. We often host events, workshops, and webinars to encourage learning and interaction. These activities not only foster a sense of belonging but also provide a chance to network with professionals and enthusiasts in the field.

By becoming a contributor, you also gain access to a wealth of learning resources. We aim to support your growth as a contributor and as a professional. Whether you're looking to deepen your understanding of specific technologies or gain insight into best practices in project development, we are committed to providing you with the tools and resources necessary for your success.

Lastly, thank you once again for your interest in contributing to Speckle. We are excited to see what you will bring to the table, and we look forward to collaborating with you as we work together to build an exceptional platform. Your contributions are not just welcome; they are essential to our mission. Together, let's innovate, create, and push the boundaries of what is possible.
